NOB is a fashion agency and buyers showroom based in the heart of Moscow. Our agency provides brand assistance and full support at every key stage of brand development including strategy formation, manufacturing and communication with fashion media, and wholesale and retail distribution. Our buyers showroom is focused on the sale and presentation of designer collections to retail partners. We also provide assistance to young designers throughout the entire brand creation, design, PR, and sales process.
